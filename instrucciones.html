Instructions



1.Click on "Play"
2.After you enter the game, you will be prompted with the question: "Can you guess the word?"
3.Below the question, you will find a box with the word "HINT"
4. Put your mouse over the box in order to read the hint about the word you are guessing (if you want to listen the hint, press the sound icon)
5.After reading the hint, select the letters that form the name of the occupation that you think is the correct one
6.To select any letter, click on the button containing the letter you want
7.You will have as many turns as there are letters in each word. (For example, a seven-letter word will give you 7 turns, and a four-letter word will give you 4 turns)
8.When you successfully guess the word or you run out of turns, you will be prompted with a windows in which you can see your score. After that, you will be prompted with a windows in which you can find the word, a sentence using that word and an image of the word you were guessing
