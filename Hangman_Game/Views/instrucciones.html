Instructions



1.Click on "Play"
2.After you enter the game, you will be prompted with the question: "Can you guess the word?"
3.Below the question, you will find a box with a hint about the occupation you are guessing
4.After reading the hint, select the letters that form the name of the occupation that you think is the correct one
5.To select any letter, click on the button containing the letter you want
6.If the letter that you selected is correct or incorrect, you will prompted with a message telling you so
7.You will have as many turns as there are letters in each word. (For example, a seven-letter word will give 7 turns, and a four-letter word will give you 4 turns)
8.When you successfully guess the word or you run out of turns, you will be prompted with a windows in which you can find the word, a sentence using that word and an image of the word you were guessing
